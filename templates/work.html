Power of your password

Yes, this is the most secur way to checked if your password has been ever chacked!!
All the time there are situations where passwords, ussers names and emails are leaking caused by hackers attacks. Even such a big companies like Facebook, Linkedin, Yahoo or Xiaomi hed their databases breached by hackers attacks.Thanks to this valuable personal data became public and hackers use them to create powerful databases and esentialy try to login to different services.
My program has benn build based on page "haveibeenpwned.com" created by security researcher Troy Hunt.
Yes... You can ask me why can't we simply get to this website and check our password. The answer is simple, because even if the site have all security certifactes I don't want to put my valuable passwords through the internet and unknown servers.
Instead of this my program use tehniqu called "K anonymity". In this case it means that your password stays on your comuter, only small hashed part of paswword (5 from 40 signs of code) goes to internet and from all answers whitch came back from server program choose only the one whitch match.
